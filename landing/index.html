FAQ

How is this different from Jobscan, Teal, or Huntr?

Those tools optimize your artifacts — resume keywords, LinkedIn profile, application volume. JobOffer Scanner evaluates the job itself: whether the company is stable, whether the pay is fair for the location, and whether you're actually a fit — before you invest the evening. Tailoring and tracking are included, but the verdict comes first.

What does “cannot fabricate” actually mean?

After the AI drafts a tailored CV or cover letter, a deterministic (non-AI) check compares every skill, year, percentage and quantitative claim in the output against your master CV, your project gallery, and the job posting. Anything new is flagged for your review. The check is code, not a prompt — it can't be sweet-talked.

Where does the company data come from?

News and layoff signals from structured sources where available, with an AI-directed web-search fallback for companies those sources miss. Every briefing labels what's sourced vs. inferred and tells you what to verify yourself. It's a research head-start, not an oracle.

Do you store my CV? Can I delete everything?

Your master CV lives in your account, encrypted in transit and access-scoped to you. You can export your full data as JSON or delete the account (password-confirmed) at any time — deletion cascades through everything.

Can I self-host it?

Yes — the project runs self-hosted with Docker Compose, with no plan limits when billing isn't configured. The hosted version is for people who'd rather not run Postgres on a Tuesday night.
