Content you submit for analysis (postings, your CV) is sent to AI model providers (e.g. Anthropic or OpenAI) acting as our processors under their business terms, which exclude training on your data. Generated documents are checked and stored in your account; nothing you submit is used to train models by us.

4. Sub-processors

Hosting/database (our cloud provider), AI model providers (analysis), Stripe (payments), email delivery (transactional mail). A current list is available on request.

5. Retention & deletion

Your data is retained while your account exists. You can export everything as JSON and delete your account (password-confirmed) at any time from account settings — deletion is immediate and cascades through applications, CVs, artifacts, and tokens. Backups age out on a fixed schedule (≤30 days).

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g. GDPR/CCPA): access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, and objection. The export and delete tools in account settings cover access, portability, and erasure self-service; for anything else email us and we'll respond within 30 days.

7. Security

Passwords, reset tokens, backup codes, and API tokens are stored hashed (bcrypt); two-factor secrets are envelope-encrypted; cross-account access is isolation-tested; transport is TLS. No system is perfectly secure — if we learn of a breach affecting your data we will notify you without undue delay.

8. Cookies & analytics

The app uses session state strictly necessary for sign-in. The marketing site uses privacy-respecting, cookieless analytics (aggregate page counts only). We do not run advertising trackers.

9. Changes

Material changes will be announced in-app or by email before taking effect.
